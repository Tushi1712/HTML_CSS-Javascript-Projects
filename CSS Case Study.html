Tourist Places in Karnataka

Here are some of the top tourist places in Karnataka: Hampi: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the former capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, with ruins of temples and forts Dandeli: A wildlife enthusiast's paradise with dense forests, limestone caves, and serene trails Nagarhole National Park: A premier tiger reserve with waterfalls, streams, lakes, hills, and rich flora and fauna Mysore: The cultural capital of Karnataka Coorg: A scenic hill station in Karnataka Udupi: The final resting place of Lord Krishna Murudeshwar: A quiet beach town by the Arabian Sea Kabini: A tourist favorite with camping options, a host of activities, and perfect weather Khwaja Banda Nawaz Darga: A famous dargah of the Sufi saint Syed Mohammad Gesu Daraz, also known as Khwaja Bande Nawaz Sri Chamundeshwari Temple: A Hindu temple on a scenic hilltop with panoramic city vistas and sunset views Siddganga Mutt: A famous place in Tumkur,known for knowledge and sacrecacy Jog Falls: The beautiful falls in the Western Ghats

    Mini Scottland of Karnataka: Coorg

    Coorg in Karnataka is often recognised as the “Scotland of India”. The place is a picturesque hill station in the southwestern part of India and is officially known as Kodagu. It has earned this title for its stunning landscapes, mist-covered hills, lush coffee plantations, and cool, temperate climate

    Kashmir of Karnataka

      Kodagu district is known as the "Kashmir of Karnataka"
  • Climate: Kodagu has a cool climate all year round
  • Oranges: Kodagu is also known as the "Land of Oranges" because of the abundance of oranges grown in the district. The Kodagu orange is unique because it is easy to peel
  • Coffee: Kodagu is known for its world-class coffee plantations
  • Wildlife: Kodagu has diverse wildlife
  • Waterfalls and peaks: Kodagu has several famous waterfalls and peaks, and hiking trails
  • History: Kodagu has a rich history, including the fort band museum
  • Other specialties: Kodagu is also known for its local chocolates and homemade wines
